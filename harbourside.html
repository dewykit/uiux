Overview

Harbourside Shopping Centre redevelopment aims to replace the current tenancies and provide 10,000sq m retail area, 33,500sqm of office space and 42,000sq m of residential space, which will contribute to a diverse and flexible housing market on the outskirts of Sydney's central business district.

Problem Statement

Design an amenity level in a mixed-use building that is accessible and usable for all users, including residents, office workers and visitors, whilst considering the demographic diversity of the space and uses. To provide adequate swimming facilities, changing rooms, restrooms, emergency evacuation routes, meeting rooms, and dining spaces to meet the need of each user group and situations. Ensuring the user experience is safe and comfortable.
