Overview

The absence of lyrics feature on the Musik platform found users having difficulties understanding and connecting with the songs they listen to, leading to lower engagement and user satisfaction levels.

Target Audience

Young adults aged between 16 and 21
iOS users


Previous Design

Current design as it stands today, the platform prominently displays the album cover, title, and the artist of the current songs playing. Additionally, the design features a clear time stamp of the song in a bar, providing users with a sense of the song's progress and duration.



Recommended Design

• I have expanded the album cover to clearly differentiate user's experience depending on the song they are listening to. Background blur was used to better the readability of the lyrics.
• The lyric feature was added, as a rolling motion as the song progresses. The current verses are highlighted in bold.
• Detail information of the song as been minimised to set clear visual hierarchy, focusing on the added feature, without compromising easy access to navigate.
