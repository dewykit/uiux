About me

As a designer, I have a curious personality and a passion for designing solutions. I believe that the design process is an ongoing dialogue that enables a product to evolve alongside the shifting landscape of the users and environment.

My background in architectural design has equipped me with valuable transferable skills that are applicable in product design. From problem-solving to spatial thinking, I've been able to leverage my experience to create effective digital experiences.


As a hobbyist, I find joy in gardening and nurturing plants. Currently, I am growing four Avocado trees, two Ground Gourds, seven Luffa vines, and one Cystal Apple Cucumber.

During my free time, I also enjoy enhancing the performance of my 3D printer by upgrading or replacing its components, and learning mark-up languages such as HTML, CSS and JavaScript to develop my own websites.


As a colleague, my colleagues have commented on my technical and visualisation skills. My work is highly regarded for its ability to effectively convey narrative elements within large-scale frameworks.

Overall, I am described as an inquisitive and proactive individual, with an exceptional eye for detail.
