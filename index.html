About me

As a desiger, I am curious for people and have a passion for designing solutions. I believe designing is an ongoing dialogue that enables a product to evolve alongside the shifting landscape of the users and the environment.

Having pursued both a Master's degree and an undergraduate degree in Architecture at University of Technology Sydney, in my previous career I worked as an architectural designer - contributing in shaping the contemporary cityscape of Sydney of today.

As an individual, I have a keen interest in exploring my creative side and keeping pace with emerging technologies. During my free time, I enjoy enhancing the performance of my 3D printer by upgrading or replacing its components, and learning mark-up languages such as HTML, CSS and JavaScript to develop my own websites. I have a tendency to delve deep into my areas of interest, often spending hours researching and learning about how things work in a systematic way.

As a hobbyist, I find joy in gardening and nurturing plants. Currently, I am growing four Avocado trees, two Ground Gourds, seven Luffa vines, and one Cystal Apple Cucumber.

As a colleague, my co-workers and supervisors have commented on my technical and visualisation skills. My supervisors have noted my attention to detail and my work is highly regarded for its ability to effectively convey narrative elements within large-scale frameworks and my capacity for understanding complex human user elements within the given ecology is particularly noteworthy. Overall, I am described as an inquisitive and proactive individual, with an exceptional eye for detail.
